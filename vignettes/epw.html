‘I have no quarrel with your decision to close the correspondence on the editorial policy of the EPW (in fact, I suggested it.)’

So wrote Kumar (“On "EPW"”), in response to a somewhat bitter exchange beginning that Kumar (“Hegemonised!”) began, to which (P.) Radhakrishnan and (T.N.) Srinivasan responded. The <em>EPW</em> rather characteristically permitted dozens of correspondents to conclude the exchange on their own pages (Chandra et al.).
